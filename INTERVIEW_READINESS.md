# Interview Readiness Guide – AI Infrastructure Engineer

## Purpose
Convert the repository’s engineer-level modules into interview preparation sessions. Focus on stories that show platform ownership, automation, and cross-team collaboration.

## Core Competencies to Highlight
- CI/CD pipelines and GitOps at scale  
- Service-level observability and incident response  
- Infrastructure-as-code design and review processes  
- Partnering with product/data teams to deliver infra features

## Preparation Checklist
1. Map each module to a competency (CI/CD, observability, cost control, etc.).  
2. Choose 3–4 STAR stories demonstrating ownership, impact, and lessons learned.  
3. Gather artefacts: pipeline configs, dashboards, decision logs stored in `projects/`.  
4. Rehearse explanations of trade-offs (tool selection, rollout strategy, guardrails).

## Mock Interview Structure (45 minutes)
| Segment | Objective | Repository Tie-In |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 min | Introduction + career arc | Root README |
| 15 min | Deep dive on a pipeline or automation project | `projects/` specs |
| 10 min | Troubleshooting scenario (SLO breach, deployment failure) | Observability modules |
| 10 min | Cross-functional collaboration example | Stakeholder sections in module notes |
| 5 min | Questions for interviewer | Prepared list below |

## Sample Technical Prompts
- “Explain how you’d design a safe rollback strategy for a platform service.”  
- “Describe the CI/CD workflow you implemented and how you measured success.”  
- “How do you monitor cost and capacity while shipping features quickly?”

Reference specific exercises or artefacts (e.g., `lessons/mod-004/`, `projects/project-02/`) to anchor answers.

## Behavioral Prompts
- Handling conflicting priorities between infra and feature teams  
- Managing incidents and follow-up actions  
- Mentoring peers or junior engineers

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer
- “How is infrastructure work prioritised relative to product roadmaps?”  
- “What metrics or dashboards do you track to gauge platform health?”  
- “How do you evaluate success for infrastructure engineers during reviews?”

## Next Steps
Document favourite stories in a personal copy of this guide, attach supporting files, and refresh after each new project or module completion.***
